Bookings & Pricing

You can easily book your session online through the Reservio booking system. After clicking the button below, you will be redirected to the calendar where you can choose an available time and the length of your session.

What the price includes

organic Japanese green tea (or another organic tea – fruit or herbal) or water
materials for non-verbal techniques (art supplies, sand, etc.)
relaxation aids for comfortable release during the session
additional materials to support therapy and further personal growth

Session length and prices

Standard session – 50 minutes

Single session: 1,900 CZK
5-session package: 9,000 CZK (1,800 CZK per session)
10-session package: 17,000 CZK (1,700 CZK per session, one session free)

Extended session – 75 minutes

Single session: 2,500 CZK
5-session package: 12,000 CZK (2,400 CZK per session)
10-session package: 22,500 CZK (2,250 CZK per session, one session free)

Language and format

Sessions take place in person and are conducted in Czech. Basic communication and arrangements are also possible in English if needed.

Cancellation policy

Cancellations or changes made less than 48 hours before the scheduled session are charged as a full session. For Monday appointments, please make any changes by Thursday of the previous week. This fee covers the reservation of time, room costs, and capacity planning.

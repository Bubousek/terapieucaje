Booking & Pricing

You can easily book your session online via the Reservio booking system. After clicking the button below, you will be redirected to the calendar, where you can choose an available time and the length of your session.

Book a Session

What is included

Organic Japanese green tea (or another organic tea – fruit or herbal) or water.
Tools for non-verbal techniques (art materials, sand, etc.).
Relaxation aids for a calm and comfortable session.
Supplementary materials supporting the therapeutic process and personal growth.

Session length & prices

Standard session – 50 minutes
Single session: 1,900 CZK
Package of 5 sessions: 9,000 CZK (1,800 CZK per session)
Package of 10 sessions: 17,000 CZK (1,700 CZK per session, one session free)

Extended session – 75 minutes
Single session: 2,500 CZK
Package of 5 sessions: 12,000 CZK (2,400 CZK per session)
Package of 10 sessions: 22,500 CZK (2,250 CZK per session, one session free)

Language & format

Therapy takes place in person in Czech. If needed, basic communication is also possible in English.

Cancellation policy

Any cancellation or change made less than 48 hours before the scheduled session is charged as a full session. For Monday appointments, changes must be made no later than Thursday of the previous week. The fee covers the reserved time, space rental, and capacity planning.
